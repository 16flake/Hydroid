One of the vegetables that is most frequently grown hydroponically is lettuce (Lactuca sativa). Plants can be grown hydroponically without using soil. Plants can be maintained by an inert substrate or grown only in a nutrient solution (liquid culture) (aggregate culture). Guide

The water in a hydroponic system must be nutrient enhanced in order for plants grown hydroponically to receive the nutrients they require. Water that has been enhanced with nutrients needs to be regularly watched to make sure that the levels are not too high or too low, which would prevent growth. Monitor

Here, you can see all other data aside from what you can see in the Monitoring Section. This section will allow you to see all other data that is also important for growing lettuce. Checking all the data from time to time is a key for producing high quality and reliable lettuce. Data

About Us

Hydroid is an automated version of hydrophonics lettuce farming. Through the use of several electronic components such as sensors, Hydroid allows the user to monitor their hydrophonic system's pH level, water level, nutrients level, humidity, and temperature.

Through this innovation, hydrophonic lettuce farming is made more easy and provides more quality to the crops.
